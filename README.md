# Turon (Banana Lumpia)

A classic Filipino snack or dessert, Turon (also known as Banana Lumpia) is a sweet and delicious treat. This recipe provides a simple and easy way to make Turon at home.

## Description

Turon is a type of Filipino snack, also called banana lumpia due to its similarity to the popular Filipino lumpia. A typical Filipino *meryenda* and dessert, Turon has gained popularity throughout the years. This recipe will guide you through making this delightful snack.

## Ingredients

*   6 pieces bananas (saba or plantains), cut in half lengthwise
*   1 cup ripe jackfruit, sliced
*   1 1/2 cups sugar
*   12 pieces lumpia wrapper
*   2 cups cooking oil

## Procedures

1.  **Sugar Coating:** Roll the banana on a plate of sugar, ensuring it's well-coated.
2.  **Wrapping:** Place the sugar-coated banana on a lumpia wrapper. Add a slice of ripe jackfruit on top.
3.  **Folding:** Fold and lock the spring roll wrapper. Use water to seal the edge.
4.  **Frying:** Heat the oil in a pan and add some sugar. Wait until the brown sugar floats.
5.  **Cooking:** Put in the wrapped banana and fry until the wrapper turns golden brown and the extra sugar sticks to the wrapper.
6.  **Serving:** Serve hot as a dessert or snack. Share and Enjoy!
